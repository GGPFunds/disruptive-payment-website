Medical Practice Payment Processing

Medical practices require payment solutions that are reliable, secure, and designed to simplify patient collections across multiple payment channels.

Disruptive Payments provides payment processing solutions for physician groups, specialty clinics, urgent care centers, concierge medicine practices, functional medicine providers, and independent medical offices throughout the United States.

Managing patient payments involves more than accepting credit cards at the front desk. Modern practices need flexible solutions that support online payments, payment plans, card on file billing, and seamless reconciliation with practice management systems.

We Support

Credit card processing
ACH payments
Virtual terminals
Online patient payments
Card on file billing
Recurring payment plans
Secure payment links
Text to pay solutions
Payment reconciliation
Point of sale integrations

Whether you operate a single location practice or a multi site physician group, Disruptive Payments can implement a payment solution that improves collections, reduces administrative burden, and creates a better experience for your patients.

Our team works directly with medical practices to understand their billing environment and identify the right combination of payment tools to support their operations.
