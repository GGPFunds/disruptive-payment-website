Government Payment Processing Solutions

Municipalities, counties, and public agencies collect revenue across dozens of categories. Utility bills, permit and license fees, court fines, parking citations, property taxes, recreation program registrations, and more all require reliable payment infrastructure that residents can access easily and staff can manage without friction.

Disruptive Payments provides payment processing and collections technology designed for the specific needs of local government. Our solutions modernize how agencies collect revenue, reduce the burden on administrative staff, and make the payment experience significantly easier for residents. When it is easy to pay, more people pay on time.

Solutions for Every Municipal Use Case

Utility Bill Collection

Water, sewer, trash, and electric utility billing requires a payment platform residents can use from any device. Our solutions support online portals, text-to-pay, and in-person payment acceptance to maximize on-time collection rates.

Permit & License Fees

Building permits, business licenses, parking permits, and other municipal fees can be collected digitally, reducing counter traffic and processing time for both residents and staff.

Court Fines & Citations

Traffic fines, parking citations, and court fees are often left unpaid because the payment process is inconvenient. Our SMS payment technology puts a payment link directly in the resident's hands, dramatically improving collection rates.

Parks & Recreation

Program registrations, facility rentals, and event fees can all be managed through a single payment platform that integrates with existing registration and scheduling systems.

Tax & Assessment Collection

Property tax and special assessment payments require secure, high value transaction processing. Our solutions handle large payments reliably, with real time posting and reconciliation.

Public Transit & Parking

Parking meters, transit fare collection, and permit renewals benefit from contactless and mobile payment acceptance that speeds up transactions and reduces the need for cash handling.

MessagePay — Text-to-Pay for Government Collections

One of the most effective tools Disruptive Payments brings to municipalities is MessagePay, an SMS payment platform that allows residents to pay bills, fines, and fees directly from a text message. No app to download, no account to create, no login required. The resident receives a personalized text with a secure payment link. If a payment method is already on file, they can reply with a single word and the payment posts in real time.

For municipalities struggling with delinquent utility accounts, unpaid citations, or overdue fees, MessagePay shifts collections from a reactive process to a proactive one. Residents are reached before an account goes delinquent, reducing the cost and friction of late collections on both sides. The result is higher collection rates, less staff time spent on outreach, and a better experience for residents who simply want an easy way to pay.

A Better Experience for Residents and Staff

The payment experience a municipality offers reflects directly on how residents perceive their local government. Disruptive Payments helps agencies modernize their payment infrastructure in a way that is easy to implement, cost effective to operate, and immediately visible to residents.
