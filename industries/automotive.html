Automotive Payment Processing for Car Dealerships

Disruptive Payments provides automotive payment processing solutions designed around the unique operational needs of new and used car dealerships, independent auto dealers, and automotive service businesses.

From integrations with market-leading CDK Global platforms to stand-alone solutions enabled by supported Verifone equipment, Disruptive Payments helps sales, service, parts, and finance departments with all payment needs. Whether you operate a single rooftop or a multi-location dealership group, our merchant services are built to handle the volume, transaction types, and compliance requirements of the modern automotive retail environment.

Disruptive Payments supports direct integration with CDK Global dealer management systems, enabling seamless payment processing across your dealership's sales, service, and parts operations without disrupting existing workflows.

Payment Solutions by Department

Every department in a dealership has distinct payment needs. Our solutions are designed to serve each one.

Sales Department

Accept down payments, deposits, and vehicle purchase transactions securely and efficiently. Our solutions support large-ticket transactions with the reliability and speed dealership sales teams require.

Service Department

Streamline repair order payments at the service drive with terminal-based solutions, digital payment links, and text-to-pay options that improve throughput and customer satisfaction.

Parts Department

Process over-the-counter parts sales, wholesale parts transactions, and phone orders with flexible payment acceptance tools including virtual terminals and card-on-file solutions.

Finance & Insurance (F&I)

Support deal funding, ancillary product payments, and customer-facing finance transactions with secure, compliant payment solutions that integrate into your F&I process.

Verifone Equipment

Disruptive Payments supports Verifone payment terminals, providing dealerships with reliable, PCI-compliant hardware that handles in-person transactions across every department. Verifone devices are widely recognized across the automotive industry for their durability and compatibility with dealer management systems.
