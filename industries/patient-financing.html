Patient-Centric Financing

Disruptive Payments offers embedded patient financing solutions designed to help medspas, aesthetic practices, and wellness providers increase treatment acceptance rates while getting paid upfront.

Easier Access to Capital

Streamlined applications and inclusive approval criteria ensure more patients can secure the funding they need for their procedures, expanding your addressable patient base.

Lower Interest Rates

Competitive interest rates help patients manage the financial burden of medical and aesthetic procedures more effectively, reducing long-term costs and alleviating financial stress.

Flexibility on Fund Usage

Patients are not restricted to a single procedure. Financing can cover primary treatments as well as related expenses such as medications, follow-up care, and retail products.

How Patients Apply

The application process is simple and takes just a few minutes.

1

Complete the Application

Patients complete a quick application directly from your office, website, or via a link sent to their phone.

2

Select a Payment Option

Patients choose from available financing plans with terms and monthly payment amounts that fit their budget.

3

Accept Terms

Once a plan is selected, patients review and accept the terms. Your practice receives payment upfront.

How Financing Reaches Your Patients

Financing can be delivered through multiple channels to fit your practice workflow.

QR Code

Patients scan a QR code displayed in your office to access financing options instantly.

Text Message

Send patients a text with a link to sign up for financing in three simple steps.

Website Integration

Embed financing directly into your website or within our payment pages via API.

Back-Office

Send financing links to patients directly through your back-office system at any time.
