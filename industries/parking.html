Parking Payment Processing Solutions

Disruptive Payments provides fast, secure credit card processing solutions for parking operators managing attended locations and unattended self-serve kiosks. Whether you operate a single surface lot, a multi-level garage, or a network of parking facilities across multiple locations, our payment solutions are designed to keep transactions moving and revenue flowing.

Parking operators face a distinct set of payment challenges — high transaction volume, the need for near-instant processing, outdoor and all-weather hardware requirements, and in many cases, no attendant on site. Disruptive Payments delivers merchant services built specifically around these demands.

Attended & Unattended Solutions

Attended Parking Locations

For lots and garages with staff on site, our attended payment solutions provide fast, reliable credit and debit card processing at the point of exit or entry. Durable terminal hardware handles high transaction volumes with consistent uptime, ensuring attendants can process payments quickly without delays or system failures.

Unattended Self-Serve Kiosks

Unattended kiosk environments require payment solutions that operate reliably without on-site support. Our unattended payment technology is engineered for 24/7 operation, supporting tap-to-pay, chip, and swipe transactions with the security protocols and uptime guarantees parking operators depend on.

Parking Garages

Multi-level garages with entry and exit lanes require payment infrastructure that integrates cleanly into gate systems and parking management software. Disruptive Payments works with parking operators to implement solutions that fit within existing infrastructure without costly overhauls.

Event & Temporary Parking

Stadiums, concert venues, fairgrounds, and special events require payment solutions that scale to handle peak volume. Our mobile and portable payment options give event parking operators the flexibility to process high transaction counts quickly during peak arrival and departure windows.

Built for High-Throughput Environments

Speed and reliability are non-negotiable in parking. A slow or failed transaction creates a bottleneck that frustrates customers and backs up your entire operation. Disruptive Payments provides payment processing with fast authorization speeds and redundant connectivity options so your lanes keep moving regardless of volume or conditions.
