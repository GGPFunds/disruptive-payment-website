Retail Payment Processing & POS Solutions

Disruptive Payments provides retail merchants with a complete payment and point of sale platform designed to save time, increase revenue, and scale alongside your business. Whether you operate a single boutique or a growing franchise network, our retail solutions give your team the tools to sell smarter and manage more effectively.

Our retail POS system is built to handle the day-to-day demands of modern retail — from in-store transactions and loyalty programs to ecommerce integrations and multi-location reporting — all from a single platform.

What Sets Our Retail Platform Apart

No More Guesswork on Customer Buying Habits

Stop guessing what your customers want. Increase your average ticket by putting customers' favorite products and average spend at your employees' fingertips during the sale — giving your team real-time insight exactly when it matters most.

Customer Loyalty

A digital rewards program that communicates directly with your sales platform. Fully customizable and built to work across all of your retail locations, allowing customers to accrue points in one store and redeem them in another.

Sales Analytics

Visualize and explore your reporting through an intuitive admin portal. View real-time reports on sales, inventory, trends, and staff performance. Compare results across locations, balance inventory and staffing, and use profit reports to stock your shelves with the right products.

Franchise & Multi-Location Support

Manage your entire retail operation from a single dashboard — whether you have 2 locations or 200. Control which products each location can carry, manage royalties, and view consolidated reporting across your full network.
