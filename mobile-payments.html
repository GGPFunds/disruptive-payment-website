MessagePay: SMS Text-to-Pay

MessagePay is a loan and bill repayment platform that allows payers to make payments directly from a text message. No app to download, no account to create, no login required. Borrowers and customers receive a personalized SMS with a secure, individualized payment link. If a payment method is on file, they simply reply with one word and the payment posts in real time.

How It Works

The payer receives a personalized text message stating the amount due and a secure payment link. They tap the link or reply to pay. If their card or bank account is already on file, a single-word reply processes the payment instantly. The entire interaction takes less than 30 seconds and requires nothing more than a phone.

For organizations sending the messages, MessagePay shifts collections from a reactive process to a proactive one. Reminders go out days before a payment is due, not after it is missed. The result is dramatically higher payment rates, lower call center volume, and a better experience for everyone involved.

Digital Payment Channels

Beyond SMS, Disruptive Payments delivers a full suite of digital collection tools so businesses can reach payers through whatever channel works best for them.

Text-to-Pay

Send personalized payment requests via SMS. Customers pay with a tap or a one-word reply. No app required. Ideal for recurring billing, overdue accounts, and appointment-based payments.

Email Invoice Payments

Deliver branded invoices by email with a built-in pay button. Customers click, review the amount, and pay in seconds. Reduces the lag between invoicing and receipt.

IVR Phone Payments

Automated phone payment collection for customers who prefer to pay by phone without speaking to an agent. Available 24/7, reducing call center load while keeping payment options accessible.

QR Code Payments

Generate a QR code that links directly to a payment page. Print it on invoices, receipts, signage, or packaging. Customers scan and pay instantly from their phone.

Dynamic Payment Pages

Branded hosted payment pages deployable in minutes. No custom development required. Ideal for campaigns, events, portals, or any situation where a dedicated payment URL is needed.

Mobile App Payments

A full-featured mobile application available on iOS and Android for businesses that need to accept payments on the go. Includes reporting, reconciliation, email receipts, and surcharging.

Alternative Payment Capabilities

Credit and debit cards are not the only way customers want to pay. Disruptive Payments supports a broad range of alternative payment methods and infrastructure tools to expand your acceptance capabilities and reduce processing costs.

ACH & eCheck

Direct bank transfers for large ticket transactions, recurring billing, and B2B payments where card processing fees are not practical. Lower cost with reliable settlement times.

Compliant Surcharging

Pass credit card processing fees to customers in a fully compliant way across card-present and card-not-present environments, reducing your net cost of acceptance.

Contactless & Tap-to-Pay

Accept Apple Pay, Google Pay, and tap-enabled cards across in-person and mobile payment environments. Faster checkouts and improved customer experience at the point of sale.

Virtual Cards

Issue single-use or multi-use virtual card numbers for vendor and supplier payments within an accounts payable automation program. Reduces fraud exposure and earns rebates on card spend.

Recurring Billing

Automated subscription and installment billing with tokenization and account updater functionality. Reduces failed payments and keeps recurring revenue flowing without manual intervention.

Account Updater

Automatically refreshes stored card credentials when a customer receives a new or replacement card, eliminating a common cause of failed recurring payments.
