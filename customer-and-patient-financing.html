Why Offer Financing

Increase Average Ticket Size

When customers can spread a purchase across monthly payments, they are more likely to choose the premium option, add services, or complete a treatment plan they might otherwise defer or decline.

Get Paid Upfront

Your business receives full payment at the time of the transaction. You carry no repayment risk. The financing partner manages collections, and you focus on delivering great service.

Expand Your Customer Base

Inclusive approval criteria and streamlined applications mean more customers qualify. Financing opens your services to people who want what you offer but need a payment structure that fits their budget.

Up to $15,000 at 0% Interest

Qualified customers can finance up to $15,000 with no interest. That is a powerful offer to put in front of someone who wants what you provide but is hesitating on the cost. Zero interest financing closes deals that would otherwise walk out the door.

Flexibility on Fund Usage

Financing is not limited to a single item or service. Customers can use approved funds across related purchases, upgrades, add-ons, and follow-up services in a single financing event.

Easier Access to Capital

Streamlined applications and fast decisions mean customers can secure financing at the point of sale without friction, keeping momentum in the conversation and reducing walk-aways.

Patient Financing for Healthcare & Medspas

For healthcare practices, medspas, and wellness providers, financing is one of the highest-impact tools available to increase treatment acceptance. When a patient is told what a procedure costs, the decision often comes down to whether they can manage that cost in their current budget. Financing changes that calculation entirely.

Our patient financing solutions are embedded directly into the practice workflow. Patients can apply during a consultation, from the waiting room, or from a follow-up text sent after their visit. Approval is fast and the terms are competitive. Your practice receives full payment at the time of service and carries no repayment risk. The result is higher case acceptance, larger average treatment values, and a better patient experience from the very first financial conversation.
