Payments Built Into the Software You Already Use

Most businesses already have software they depend on — a point of sale system, an EMR or EHR platform, a practice management tool, or an ecommerce storefront. The question is whether payment processing works seamlessly inside that software or creates friction around it.

Disruptive Payments integrates payment acceptance directly into your existing platforms. Our team works with you to identify the right integration path, connect your payment infrastructure to your software environment, and ensure your team can accept payments without ever leaving the system they already know. If you are not sure whether your platform is compatible, reach out and we will find out together.

Integration Categories

Point of Sale Systems

Whether you are running a retail POS, a restaurant system, or an industry-specific terminal setup, we integrate payment processing so transactions flow directly through your existing hardware and software without a separate processing layer.

EMR & EHR Platforms

Healthcare practices using electronic medical and health record systems can embed patient payment collection directly into the clinical workflow. Collect copays, balances, and deposits inside the platform your staff already uses every day.

Ecommerce Platforms

Online sellers need payment processing that connects cleanly to their storefront. We integrate with the leading ecommerce platforms so your checkout experience is seamless, secure, and fully under your control.

Practice Management Software

Dental, legal, veterinary, and other service-based practices often run industry-specific practice management tools. We work with those platforms to embed billing and payment collection where it fits naturally into your workflow.

CRM & ERP Systems

Businesses running CRM or ERP platforms can embed payment acceptance and AP automation directly into those systems, eliminating manual data entry and keeping payment data synchronized with your records automatically.

Custom & Proprietary Software

If your business runs on a platform built specifically for your operation, we can work with your development team or ours to build a payment integration that fits. Our gateway partners provide robust APIs for custom environments.

Ecommerce Integrations via Authorize.net

For businesses selling online, Disruptive Payments leverages the Authorize.net gateway to connect payment processing directly to the most widely used ecommerce platforms. Authorize.net has pre-built integrations that make setup straightforward, with no custom development required for standard configurations.

Through Authorize.net, we support payment integrations for WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and WordPress, covering the majority of independent online storefronts. If your ecommerce platform is not listed, contact our team and we will confirm compatibility before you get started.

WooCommerce

Full payment processing integration for WordPress-based online stores running WooCommerce.

BigCommerce

Native Authorize.net integration for BigCommerce storefronts of any size.

WordPress

Accept payments on WordPress sites through Authorize.net-compatible payment plugins.
